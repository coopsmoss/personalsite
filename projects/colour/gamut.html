STANDARD Color Wheel

YURMBY Color Wheel

Yellow, green cyan, blue, magenta and red (and all their intermediaries). This color wheel combines the RBG with CMY color wheel and is a truer representation of how we perceive color. Yurmby color wheel was created by Tobey Sanford www.greatpix.com/tobey/biography.html
